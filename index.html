What is Internet Safety?



Curiosity! Excellent, you are asking a great question!

What really is Internet Safety? Well... basically it is the discipline of adhering to practical principles, comprehending current technology, and safeguarding your digital equipment in order to protect yourself from the harmful aspects of the online world.

Sounds important, right?
Well that's because it is!

... but why?

That's because searching for answers to queries, instructions on how to do a task, and other things on the internet delivers instant gratification. It is, nevertheless, full with potential risks. Malware, phishing, frauds, malicious files, distortion, and misinformation lurk in every nook and cranny of the internet, waiting for a web user to check them out.

It sounds scary, but you can fight against these murky nuisance with knowledge! It is amazing what education can do. Learning how to combat the murky nuisance will definitely aid you in the acquisition of information throughout the web.

Know how to stay safe online!

Being connected to your friends, family, and everyone else online can be awesome! But nothing we do online is really private, including social media, text messages and even the apps you use on your own phone!

Do not worry, you are in control of how much or how little you want to share with your friends, your networks, and the world. As long as you know how to use social media and the internet safely, you can protect yourself!
