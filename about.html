About Us

Welcome to Tap and Lock, your trusted source for information. We are dedicated to giving you authentic information, with a focus on internet safety.

Tap and Lock is a passion project that aims to educate web users about internet safety and how they can avoid them. We provide a compilation of details from trusted and verified sources to ensure that users will get quality information. Online misinformation drove us to do research and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration to create Tap and Lock. We serve users all over the web, and are thrilled to be a part of combatting online misinformation and cyber crimes.

We hope you learn more about internet safety as much as we enjoy offering them to you.

Mission

Our mission is to organize information and make it useful. Our commitment will be to help everyone find the information they need about internet safety.

Vision

Our vision is to have a safer environment for the youth in the web. Tap and Lock will be a place in which children learn about internet safety.
